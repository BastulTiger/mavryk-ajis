Powering the $MVRK Economy
The MVRK token fuels the Mavryk ecosystem, securing the network through staking and validator nodes. With automated fee burns and built-in economic incentives, MVRK ensures sustainable growth and long-term value preservation.
Token Utility
Transaction Fees
MVRK tokens are used to pay for on-chain transactions, ensuring seamless, low-cost, and secure transfers across the Mavryk network. These fees help sustain network operations.
Staking & Delegation
Secure the network by staking MVRK tokens. Validators use staked tokens to process transactions and validate blocks, while delegators can stake with validators to earn passive rewards without running a node themselves.
Validator Nodes
Validators play a critical role in maintaining network security by verifying transactions and blocks, safeguarding the ecosystem against malicious activity. MVRK serves as a security deposit for nodes, ensuring network integrity.
Liquidity Mining
Earn rewards by providing liquidity to decentralized applications (dApps) and liquidity pools. By supplying MVRK tokens, users contribute to market stability and receive incentives in return.
Governance
MVRK token holders have voting power to influence key protocol decisions, such as network upgrades, fee structures, and ecosystem funding. The more tokens held, the greater the governance influence.
Treasury
The on-chain treasury collects fees and funds ecosystem development, supporting grants, protocol improvements, and strategic initiatives. This ensures long-term sustainability and innovation within the ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is $MVRK?
$MVRK is the native utility token of the Mavryk Network. It powers the ecosystem by enabling staking, securing validator nodes, paying for network transactions, and participating in governance. $MVRK also features automated fee burns and integrated economic incentives to support long-term value preservation and sustainable growth.
What is $MVRK used for?

$MVRK serves multiple roles:

  • Pays transaction fees
  • Powers staking and validation
  • Grants governance rights for treasury proposals
  • Fuels the on-chain protocol treasury
  • Enables liquidity mining, bug bounties, and more
When does the $MVRK ICO start?
The $MVRK ICO begins on May 12, 2025, at 12:00 UTC | 16:00 GST.
How much is each $MVRK token priced at?
The $MVRK token starts at $0.10 per token, with a tiered pricing structure that increases by $0.005 at each tier.
How can I participate in the $MVRK ICO?

The ICO is accessible on this page. Additionally, users can also access the token sale via the Uniswap and Aerodrome interfaces as well.

Where will the $MVRK tokens be traded?

The $MVRK tokens will be available through a custom webpage that connects to two DEXs, featuring single-sided liquidity where $MVRK will be deposited into a concentrated liquidity pool (AMM V3) on two networks:

  • USDT on Ethereum Mainnet: Purchase $MVRK with USDT via Uniswap V3
  • USDC on Base Mainnet: Purchase $MVRK with USDC via Aerodrome CLP (similar to Uniswap V3)
Which cryptocurrencies are accepted in the ICO?
The $MVRK ICO accepts USDT (on Ethereum) and USDC (on Base).
What is the total supply and ICO allocation of $MVRK?
The total supply of $MVRK is 1 billion tokens, with 100 million (10% of the supply) allocated for the ICO.
Can I migrate my $MVRK tokens between Ethereum and Base?
Yes, $MVRK is integrated with the Axelar bridge, allowing seamless migration of tokens between Ethereum and Base networks.
Where can I trade $MVRK tokens after the TGE?

$MVRK will be available for trading on major decentralized exchanges (DEXs) including Aerodrome Finance, Dexscreener, and Dextools.

What makes the Mavryk TGE “fair launch”?
Mavryk’s ICO follows a classic, DEX-style fair launch approach. We are not pairing tokens to a stablecoin or locking in any valuations before the market sets the price. This ensures that price discovery is entirely community-driven, giving everyone a fair opportunity to participate without any early insider advantages.
